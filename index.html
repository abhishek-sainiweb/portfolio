Who I am

I value human life, honest effort, and nature — in that order, always.

I come from a lower-middle-class family in India, and that shaped how I see everything. I'm a god-loving person, and I believe technology should serve people, not the other way around.

I'm convinced that a safe, sustainable future isn't only ahead of us — a lot of it lives behind us, in the roots we've drifted away from. So I try to build with that balance: forward-looking tools held to grounded values.

I'm not a traditional coder. I'm a visualizer — I see a concept clearly before it exists, then use AI as my pair to bring it to life. People around me come to me for a perspective on all sorts of things, and that range is something I lean into rather than narrow.

What guides the work

  • People firstEvery build answers to a human need before anything else.
  • Rooted in natureIf it doesn't respect the earth, it isn't progress.
  • Effort is sacredHonest work — mine and others' — is worth honoring.
  • Faith & humilityI build small, grateful, and with the long view in mind.
